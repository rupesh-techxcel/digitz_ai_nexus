How Nexus AI Works

Layered knowledge. Governed delivery. One AI platform.

Each tenant’s knowledge is separated, access-controlled, identity-qualified, and delivered through the right channel — with human escalation when AI should not continue alone.

AI Chat Bot Identity-governed conversational AI
Q&A Knowledge-sourced structured answers
Agentic Workflow Apps Purpose-built agentic AI workflows for business
First, each tenant gets a persistent knowledge layer. Business content becomes a governed knowledge system \u2014 separated by tenant, reusable across channels, and ready for AI delivery.
Then, access control becomes part of the knowledge. Knowledge Profiles expose only the safe, approved surface for each business situation.
Next, the visitor becomes an identity profile. Public, customer, employee, partner, or training identities are resolved before delivery.
Then, the chatbot follows its behaviour profile. Persona, tone, fallback rules, confidence threshold, and escalation policy guide the AI response.
When needed, AI escalates to a human. The handoff keeps tenant, identity, channel, and knowledge context so the human team can continue safely.

The Nexus Platform

The Core That Makes Everything Possible

Before any chat bot responds, any Q&A answers, or any workflow app runs — three platform workspaces work together to configure, govern, and monitor every AI experience you deploy.

Explore the Nexus Platform
Studio

Nexus Studio

The authoring workspace. Design, configure, and refine every AI experience before it goes live.

  • Build and configure chat bots, Q&A flows, and workflow apps
  • Define knowledge sources, topics, and approved content
  • Set identity rules, access policies, and user categories
  • Design escalation triggers and agent routing logic
  • Preview and test AI responses before deployment
Administration

Nexus Administration

The governance workspace. Manage tenants, users, and platform-wide rules across every deployment.

  • Manage multi-tenant workspaces and tenant isolation
  • Control user roles, access levels, and permissions
  • Enforce knowledge governance and content policies
  • Audit AI decisions, responses, and escalation history
  • Configure platform settings and integration endpoints
Live

Nexus Live

The operations workspace. Monitor live conversations, handle escalations, and track platform health in real time.

  • Real-time conversation monitoring across all tenants
  • Live escalation queue with full conversation context
  • Agent console for human handover and resolution
  • Performance dashboards for response and resolution rates
  • Alerts for SLA breaches, anomalies, and volume spikes
