How It Works

How the Nexus Chat Bot Works?

From visitor interaction to human escalation, DIGITZ AI Nexus guides each conversation through the right category, the right AI agent, and the right next action.

Visitor Interaction

A visitor opens the chat and asks a specific question about pricing, products, services, support, or business information.

Category Selection

The conversation is directed through the selected category such as Sales, Product, Pricing, Support, or General Enquiry.

Designated AI Agent

DIGITZ AI Nexus assigns the chat to the most relevant AI agent based on the selected category and business context.

AI Understands & Responds

The agent identifies the intent, figures out the answer from approved context, and responds with a focused, contextual reply.

Escalation When Needed

If the query needs confirmation, deeper handling, or support intervention, the chat is escalated to a designated human agent.

Business-ready flow: Visitor asks → category selected → relevant AI agent takes over → contextual answer is delivered → human team steps in when required.
