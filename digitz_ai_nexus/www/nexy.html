What Nexy Does in Every Conversation

Nexy engages every visitor through a conversation that is simultaneously grounded in your approved business knowledge and informed by a live intelligence layer that reads what each visitor is signalling through their questions, language, and behaviour. It is not a scripted chatbot — it reasons about each visitor's position in their buying journey and responds with the precision and tone appropriate to that moment.

From the very first message, Nexy draws from your published knowledge base — your products, services, value propositions, AI personas, and playbooks — to hold conversations that represent your business accurately and persuasively. Every answer is grounded in what you have approved. Nothing is invented.

Across each message, Nexy continuously updates its understanding of the visitor: where they are in their journey, what signals they are expressing, which communication persona fits them best, and how serious their enquiry is. This intelligence shapes every response — making each conversation feel relevant, timely, and genuinely helpful to the visitor's actual situation.

The Per-Message Intelligence Loop

With every message a visitor sends, Nexy re-evaluates their journey milestone, re-checks for buying signals, re-confirms the matched persona, and updates the enquiry score. This happens before each response is generated — meaning Nexy always responds from the most current understanding of who this visitor is and where they are in their decision process. No response is generic. Each one is calibrated to this visitor, at this moment.

Knowledge-grounded responses from your approved content

Every answer Nexy gives is drawn from your published knowledge base — products, services, pricing, value propositions, and business policies. Nothing is hallucinated. Every response is accurate, on-brand, and backed by content you have reviewed and approved.

AI personas and playbooks govern tone, depth, and approach

Nexy operates through configurable AI personas — each with its own communication style, discovery approach, and playbook. The right persona activates based on the visitor's profile, ensuring that every conversation is delivered with the appropriate tone and depth for that visitor type.

Journey milestone tracking across seven stages

Nexy tracks where each visitor sits in their buying journey across seven defined milestones: Awareness, Consideration, Intent, Decision, Action, Loyalty, and Advocacy. The milestone is re-evaluated with each message and directly shapes the content, tone, and direction of every response.

Buying signal detection in real time

Nexy detects buying signals as they surface in conversation — pricing interest, direct comparison questions, timeline expressions, urgency language, and readiness cues. Each detected signal updates the visitor's signal profile and informs how urgently and directly Nexy steers the conversation toward a decision.

Visitor persona matching for tailored communication

Nexy matches each visitor to a customer persona based on their message content, stated context, and expressed intent — scoring across multiple attributes including industry, role, company profile, and buying pattern. The matched persona determines the communication approach for the remainder of the conversation, and re-matching occurs as the visitor reveals more about themselves.

Enquiry quality and intent scoring

Every conversation is scored in real time across multiple dimensions — message clarity, intent strength, expressed urgency, and information depth. The score updates with each message exchange and provides a continuous picture of how serious and qualified this visitor's enquiry is, enabling Nexy to respond proportionally and surface high-quality conversations for human attention.

Human escalation with full conversation context

When a visitor's situation calls for human expertise — a complex requirement, a high-value opportunity, or a situation where AI should step aside — Nexy escalates to a human agent on your support desk. The full conversation history, journey milestone, persona match, enquiry score, and detected signals are passed to the agent so they can pick up without asking the visitor to repeat themselves.

Meeting and consultation booking within the conversation

When a visitor is ready for a more structured conversation, Nexy presents a meeting booking option directly within the chat — rendered as a calendar booking card linked to your configured calendar. The visitor can book a slot without leaving the conversation, and no manual intervention from your team is required.

"Nexy does not just answer questions — it reads the visitor, tracks their journey, detects their signals, matches their persona, scores their intent, and calibrates every response to guide them toward a confident buying decision. All within your approved knowledge boundary. All without hallucination."

Intelligence Engine

How Nexy Understands Every Visitor

Behind every response Nexy delivers is a structured intelligence layer that processes each visitor's conversation in real time. This layer does not simply retrieve an answer — it first builds a contextual picture of the visitor and then generates a response calibrated to that picture.

The intelligence systems run on every message, continuously refining their understanding as the conversation deepens. The result is a companion that becomes more accurate and more effective as each exchange progresses — not one that simply pattern-matches keywords against a database.

Seven-Stage Journey Framework

Nexy evaluates visitor messages against a seven-stage journey model: Awareness, Consideration, Intent, Decision, Action, Loyalty, and Advocacy. The current milestone is determined by the language, questions, and context the visitor expresses — and updated on every message. Journey stage directly governs how Nexy positions information and how directly it steers toward a decision.

Multi-Signal Buying Intent Detection

Nexy scans each message for buying signals across several categories: pricing and budget expressions, direct comparison or alternative-seeking language, timeline or deadline signals, strong intent phrases, and explicit readiness cues. Detected signals accumulate a weighted profile that reflects how close the visitor is to making a buying decision — and shapes the urgency and direction of the response.

Visitor Persona Matching and Re-Matching

Nexy scores each visitor against your configured customer personas using weighted attributes — industry, company size, role, stated pain points, and buying pattern indicators. The highest-scoring persona match is applied at the start of the conversation and re-evaluated as the visitor provides more context. Persona determines the AI persona and playbook that govern the communication style for the remainder of the session.

Real-Time Enquiry Scoring

Every conversation is scored continuously across multiple dimensions — clarity of need, expressed urgency, specificity of questions, depth of engagement, and intent strength. The score is updated with every message exchange and represents a live assessment of the visitor's enquiry quality. Conversations above certain score thresholds receive higher-priority treatment, including earlier escalation offers and more direct conversion steering.

AI Persona and Playbook Governance

Nexy's responses are guided by the AI persona matched to the visitor — each persona carries a communication style profile, a discovery approach, a confidence threshold for escalation, and a playbook that defines how Nexy navigates certain types of visitor statements. Playbook rules are evaluated at each response to ensure the conversation remains within the boundaries you have configured for that visitor type.

Governed Escalation and Desk Handoff

Escalation is not reactive — it is governed by the confluence of journey milestone, enquiry score, signal strength, and persona-defined confidence threshold. When these conditions indicate that human involvement will serve the visitor better than continued AI conversation, Nexy initiates escalation — transferring the full conversation context, enriched with journey stage, persona match, score, and detected signals — to your support desk.

Same Intelligence Engine on WhatsApp

Nexy's intelligence layer — journey tracking, buying signal detection, persona matching, enquiry scoring, escalation governance — operates identically on WhatsApp as it does on your website. WhatsApp contacts receive the same knowledge-grounded, persona-calibrated responses as website visitors. The only difference is the channel. The only exception is meeting booking via calendar card, which is rendered in the website chat widget and not in WhatsApp messaging.

"Every Nexy response is the output of a system that has already evaluated where this visitor is in their journey, what buying signals they are expressing, which persona fits them best, and how their enquiry scores against your qualification criteria — before a single word is generated."
