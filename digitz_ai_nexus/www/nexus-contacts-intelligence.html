Nexus Contacts Intelligence is the centralised relationship intelligence layer of the Nexus platform. It brings all contacts, leads, prospects, customers, and business relationships into one intelligent system — enriched with context, mapped to relevant products or services, and orchestrated by Nexy across WhatsApp, email, chat, and future communication channels.

The Challenge

Contacts Are Everywhere.
Intelligence Is Missing.

Most businesses have contacts scattered across WhatsApp, email, spreadsheets, CRM systems, website forms, and chat conversations. The real challenge is not storing contacts — it is understanding them.

Who is this contact, and what is their business context?
Which product or service is most relevant to them?
What was already discussed, and what is the next step?
Which channel should be used — WhatsApp, email, or chat?
What message should be sent, and in what tone?
When is human follow-up required, and who should handle it?

Without contact intelligence, outreach becomes generic. Follow-up becomes inconsistent. Opportunities are missed.

Nexus Contacts Intelligence solves this by turning scattered contact records into structured relationship intelligence — enriched with business context, product-fit mapping, and next-best-action readiness for Nexy to activate.

Every contact becomes a structured intelligence record that Nexy can act on — with the right context, the right product fit, and the right next action already prepared.

Core Capabilities

A Unified Intelligence Layer for
Every Business Relationship.

Nexus Contacts Intelligence combines contact data, business context, product-fit mapping, Orbit knowledge, and Nexy execution into one system that helps businesses understand and act on every relationship.

Product Fit Intelligence lives inside Nexus Contacts Intelligence — helping the platform identify which product or service is most relevant to each contact, why it fits, and what action should happen next.

01
Contact Master Intelligence

Store and organise all contact details — name, email, phone, WhatsApp number, company, industry, country, role, source, interest, consent status, communication preference, and assigned Nexy profile — in one structured, searchable layer.

Every contact becomes structured, traceable, and ready for intelligent engagement.
02
Organisation & Company Context

Link contacts with their organisations. Nexus understands the business behind the person — company profile, business category, size, industry, challenges, current systems, and potential requirements.

Nexy communicates based on the business reality, not just the person's name.
03
Contact Intelligence Profile

Nexus builds an intelligence profile for each contact — identifying business type, likely persona, pain points, product interest, buying stage, engagement level, lead quality, and next best action.

Nexy communicates with relevance and purpose, not guesswork.
04
Product Fit Intelligence

Product Fit Intelligence is a sub-engine inside Nexus Contacts Intelligence. It evaluates contact profile, persona, pain points, engagement signals, and product definitions to identify the most relevant product or service for each contact — and exactly why it fits.

Contacts Intelligence decides the fit. Nexy acts on the recommendation.
05
Nexy Outreach Orchestration

Using contact intelligence, product mapping, and Orbit knowledge, Nexy prepares personalised WhatsApp messages, cold email drafts, follow-ups, reply responses, demo invitations, and re-engagement messages.

Communication powered by context, knowledge, and relationship history.
06
Reply Intelligence & Governance

When a contact replies, Nexy classifies the intent — Interested, Needs Pricing, Unsubscribe, Needs Human, and more — and prepares the right next action. Every outreach step can be controlled with approval rules, consent checks, and suppression lists.

Intelligent outreach with safety, consent, and human oversight built in.

Nexy as Agentic Candidate

Nexy Activates Contact Intelligence
Through Conversation and Action.

Nexy is the agentic candidate that uses the intelligence produced by Nexus Contacts Intelligence and the approved knowledge governed by Nexus Orbit. It does not replace the contact intelligence layer. Instead, Nexy turns contact recommendations, product-fit insights, and next-best actions into real communication across WhatsApp, email, chat, and connected business workflows.

Contacts Intelligence identifies the opportunity.

Nexus Orbit provides the knowledge.

Nexy carries the conversation forward.

01
Uses Contact Recommendations

Nexy receives contact insights, product-fit results, lead stage, and next-best-action recommendations from Nexus Contacts Intelligence — and acts on them directly.

Nexy acts on contact intelligence. It does not produce it.
02
Grounds Messages With Orbit Knowledge

Nexy uses approved product knowledge, FAQs, references, objection responses, and communication rules from Nexus Orbit to ensure every message is accurate and on-brand.

Every message Nexy sends is knowledge-grounded, not assumed.
03
Prepares Channel-Specific Communication

Nexy converts the recommended next-best action into WhatsApp messages, email drafts, chat replies, follow-up sequences, and human handover notes — tailored to the contact and channel.

Nexy converts next-best-action into real communication.
04
Handles Reply Flow

When a contact responds, Nexy manages the conversation flow, prepares suitable replies grounded in Orbit knowledge, and routes sensitive or high-value cases to the right human for follow-up.

Nexy carries the conversation. Humans step in when it matters.
05
Updates the Contact Journey

After every interaction, Nexy writes back the outcome, reply status, interest level, and recommended next action to Nexus Contacts Intelligence — keeping the contact record current and actionable.

Nexy updates the contact journey after every conversation.
06
Moves Opportunities Forward

Nexy helps move contacts from captured lead to engaged prospect, qualified opportunity, demo, proposal, or human follow-up — orchestrating the execution of each step without replacing the intelligence that defined it.

Nexy orchestrates the execution. Contacts Intelligence defines the path.
