How Governance Works

Approval Rules

Control when Nexy can send automatically and when a human must review first. Rules can be set by channel, contact stage, message type, or lead value.

Suppression and Consent

Nexy respects unsubscribe requests, do-not-contact rules, and channel eligibility settings from Contacts Intelligence — every action stays within consent boundaries.

Knowledge Grounding

Every message is drawn from approved Nexus Orbit knowledge — not from model assumptions. Accuracy, consistency, and brand alignment across every contact.

Human Handover

Complex or high-value conversations escalate to the right human with complete context — journey stage, product-fit, reply classification, and next-best-action all transferred.
