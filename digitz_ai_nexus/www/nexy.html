Agentic Growth Action

Nexy Turns Contact Intelligence Into Real Business Conversations.

Nexy does not replace Nexus Contacts Intelligence. Instead, Nexy activates it.

Nexus Contacts Intelligence identifies who the contact is, what they may need, which product or service fits them, and what action should happen next. Nexus Orbit provides the approved business knowledge that can be used in the communication.

Nexy takes those recommendations and carries the conversation forward. It prepares the right message, adapts it for the right channel, responds to replies, asks follow-up questions, triggers human review when needed, and updates the contact journey after every interaction.

Nexy is not another intelligence layer. Nexy is the agentic candidate that converts intelligence into communication, action, and business movement. Contacts Intelligence decides the fit. Nexus Orbit supplies the proof. Nexy carries the conversation.
Core Capabilities

What Nexy Does Across Every Contact and Channel.

Uses Contact Recommendations

Nexy receives contact insights, product-fit results, buying stage, lead score, communication eligibility, and next-best-action recommendations from Nexus Contacts Intelligence. It acts on these inputs. It does not produce them.

Grounds Every Message With Orbit

Nexy uses Nexus Orbit to access approved product knowledge, service details, FAQs, pricing notes, references, objection handling, and communication rules. Every message Nexy sends is knowledge-grounded, not assumed.

Prepares Channel-Specific Communication

Nexy converts recommendations into WhatsApp messages, email drafts, chat replies, follow-up notes, proposal reminders, demo invitations, and CRM handover messages adapted to each channel's format and tone.

Conducts the Conversation

Nexy manages the live communication flow. It can ask relevant questions, clarify requirements, answer from approved knowledge, and keep the conversation aligned with the contact's stage and product-fit recommendation.

Handles Replies and Objections

When a contact replies, Nexy prepares suitable responses based on the reply type, contact context, product-fit recommendation, and approved knowledge from Nexus Orbit. It carries the conversation without losing thread continuity.

Updates the Contact Journey

After each interaction, Nexy writes back the outcome, reply status, interest level, next action, and handover requirement to Nexus Contacts Intelligence. Nexy updates the contact journey. It does not define it.

Moves Opportunities Forward

Nexy helps move contacts from captured lead to engaged prospect, qualified opportunity, demo, proposal, customer, or nurture stage. Nexy orchestrates the execution. Contacts Intelligence defines the path.

Supports Human-Governed Action

Nexy can route sensitive replies, pricing discussions, high-value leads, complaints, or uncertain cases to human review before action is taken — transferring full contact context and conversation history to the right person.

Human Governance

Agentic Action With Human Governance.

Nexy is designed to act within business-approved boundaries. It can prepare messages, suggest actions, and continue conversations — but the business decides which actions need human approval, which channels are eligible for each contact, and which conversations should escalate immediately to a human.

Approval Rules

Control when Nexy can send automatically and when a human must review and approve the prepared message before it is delivered. Approval rules can be set by channel, contact stage, message type, or lead value.

Suppression and Consent

Nexy respects unsubscribe requests, do-not-contact rules, channel eligibility settings, and communication preference records from Nexus Contacts Intelligence — ensuring every outreach action is within consent and suppression boundaries.

Knowledge Grounding

Every message Nexy prepares is drawn from approved Nexus Orbit knowledge — not from model assumptions or unverified inference. This ensures accuracy, consistency, and brand alignment across every contact and every channel.

Human Handover

Complex, sensitive, or high-value conversations are escalated to the right human with complete contact and conversation context — including journey stage, product-fit recommendation, reply classification, and next-best-action from Contacts Intelligence.
