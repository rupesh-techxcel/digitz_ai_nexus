How It Works

How Nexus Chat Works

From visitor interaction to human escalation, DIGITZ AI Nexus guides each conversation through the right channel, category, identity, AI agent profile, access policy, approved knowledge, and next action.

Channel Interaction

A public visitor, verified customer, or internal user opens chat and asks a question about products, support, training, SOPs, ERP tasks, or business information.

Category Selection

The conversation is directed through the selected chat category such as Sales, Product, Pricing, Support, Training, SOP, Internal Knowledge, or ERP Assistance.

Verified Access

Nexus resolves identity, access category, and access policy so the user receives only the knowledge, tools, and actions they are authorized to use.

AI Agent Responds

The AI Agent Profile identifies intent, retrieves approved knowledge, prepares a focused response, and can use approved tools where the business process allows it.

Escalation When Needed

If the query needs confirmation, deeper handling, approval, or support intervention, the chat is escalated to the designated human team with the conversation context.

Business-ready flow: User asks -> category selected -> identity and policy verified -> AI agent responds from approved knowledge or tools -> human team steps in when required.

Designated Chat Agent

What does a Designated Chat Agent mean?

A Designated Chat Agent is the configured AI agent experience assigned to a specific chat category. It defines how the conversation should behave, what knowledge it can use, what tone it should follow, and when the interaction should move to a human team or an approved business tool.

Behaviour

Controls how the agent responds

The agent can be shaped for sales, support, training, SOP guidance, ERP assistance, or internal knowledge. Its behaviour includes response style, level of detail, follow-up questions, escalation rules, and the preferred next action.

Knowledge Boundary

Uses only the approved context

The designated agent does not answer from a random pool of information. It works with the approved knowledge sources, access category, and access policy resolved for the user and selected chat category.

Business Role

Acts as the specialist for that route

A pricing enquiry can use a commercial agent, a support query can use a support agent, and an internal SOP question can use an operational guidance agent. Each route gets a focused specialist instead of one generic chatbot.

Escalation and Tools

Knows when to stop or hand over

The agent can be configured to stop at approved answers, ask for confirmation, prepare a ticket, escalate to a human team, or use approved ERP and business tools when the policy permits it.
