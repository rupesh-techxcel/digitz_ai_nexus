Common Questions

Questions about pricing

Still deciding? Here are the answers we hear most often.

What does the onboarding fee cover?

The one-time onboarding fee covers environment setup, knowledge base structuring, channel configuration, identity policy setup, and a guided launch session with your team. It ensures your platform is production-ready from day one — not just installed.

Can I upgrade from Basic to Premium later?

Yes — plan upgrades are prorated. Your knowledge base, conversations, and configurations carry over without any migration work. Upgrades typically activate within one business day.

What counts as an "interaction"?

An interaction is a complete visitor conversation session — regardless of the number of messages within it. Agentic background tasks (CRM sync, workflow steps) do not count toward your interaction quota.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a guided 14-day proof-of-concept for qualified organisations. During PoC you get a fully configured Basic environment with your own knowledge content loaded — so you evaluate with real data, not demo data.

Does the Basic plan support the website chat widget?

Yes. All plans include the embeddable website chat widget. Register your domain once in the admin panel and the widget loads on every page of that domain automatically — one script tag, site-wide coverage.

How does Advanced custom pricing work?

Advanced pricing is scoped to your deployment — tenant count, expected interaction volume, infrastructure requirements, custom workflow complexity, and integration depth. Reach out via the chat and we'll turn around a proposal within 48 hours.
